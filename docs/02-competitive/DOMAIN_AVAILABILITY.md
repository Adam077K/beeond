## TL;DR recommendation

- **Primary: `beeond.ai`** — available, and the strongest possible TLD for an AI-native brand. Register immediately.
- **Fallback / defensive: `beeond.io`** (available) and **`beeond.agency`** (likely available) — grab to protect the brand and for redirects.
- **`beeond.com`** is **taken but for sale at $3,795** (HugeDomains). Worth acquiring *later*, once revenue justifies it, for email trust + type-in traffic — not a day-one bootstrapped spend.
- **Brand-collision caution:** two unrelated "Beeond" entities already exist — **Beeond, Inc.** (IIoT/OPC-UA software, US; owns `beeond.net` + `linkedin.com/company/beeond`) and **Beeond Publicidade** (a Brazilian advertising company; owns `@beeond` on Instagram). Neither competes in HE/EN B2B-SaaS marketing, but the bare `@beeond` handle is gone on at least two networks. **Recommend a consistent disambiguated handle** (e.g. `@beeondhq`, `@getbeeond`, or `@beeondai`) across all social — verify the chosen variant before committing.

## Recommendation rationale

1. **`beeond.ai` as primary** is on-strategy: the brand's core promise is AI-native delivery, and `.ai` signals exactly that to the B2B/SaaS/tech ICP. It is available right now.
2. **Don't fight for the bare `@beeond` socials.** With an IIoT company on LinkedIn and a Brazilian ad agency on Instagram already holding the name, a clean disambiguated handle used consistently everywhere is better for brand recall and avoids confusion in the marketing/advertising-adjacent space. Decide the variant (`@beeondhq` reads cleanest for B2B) and lock it across all five networks the same day.
3. **Acquire `beeond.com` opportunistically.** $3,795 is a real but recoverable cost; for a bootstrapped two-founder team it is not a week-one priority. Launch on `.ai`, revisit `.com` once a paying retainer lands (it improves cold-email deliverability and captures type-in/"just-google-it" traffic).
4. **Defensive registrations:** grab `beeond.io` and `beeond.agency` (both cheap, both available) to prevent a competitor or squatter from muddying the brand while it is new.
