# Copy Strategy — Hebrew-first, English parity

*Start from zero. Less is more — measured in what we say and how, not word count. Hebrew is the source language; English is a crafted parallel, not a translation. All seed copy below is a **candidate** for Session B + CMO to refine and lock, and every Hebrew line needs a native-HE read before shipping.*

---

## Voice (four dials)

1. **Confident, not loud.** We state claims as facts with tension. No hype adjectives ("revolutionary," "cutting-edge"). The reference sites earn belief with specificity, not volume.
2. **Human, founder-led.** Two real people are behind this. The copy sounds like Yarden and Adam talking, not a brand committee. Warm-tech, not corporate.
3. **Anti-generic, on purpose.** The one thing we can never sound like is "any AI marketing agency." If a sentence could appear on a competitor's site, delete it. Our whole promise is that AI *won't* make you generic — the copy must prove it.
4. **Plain, then sharp.** Short declaratives. One idea per sentence. A single sharp line beats three hedged ones.

---

## Less-is-more rules (enforced in the B3 copy pass)

- Every section gets **one message**. If you're writing a second, it's a second section or it's cut.
- Kill throat-clearing ("In today's fast-paced world…"). Start at the verb.
- No feature lists where an outcome sentence does the job.
- Cut every adjective that isn't doing work. Read it aloud; if you'd never say it to a customer's face, rewrite.
- The **CTA is always specific**: *the Footprint Audit* — never "Get Started."
- Numbers > claims. When we have proof, lead with it. Until then, be honest about "first cohort" — honesty *is* the anti-generic move.

---

## The message hierarchy (what we're actually saying, in order)

1. **The world changed** — buyers (and the AIs they ask) evaluate you across many channels, fast.
2. **Most brands are losing there** — invisible or generic where it counts.
3. **Beeond runs the whole footprint as one swarm** — every channel, coordinated, AI-native, founder-led.
4. **The proof of not-generic** — automation does the volume; humans do the taste. Here's how it works, phased and honest.
5. **The obvious next step** — a free Footprint Audit that shows you exactly where you stand.

## Seed copy — HE (primary) + EN (parity)

> These are directional candidates to spark the B3 pass, not locked copy. Pick, sharpen, and stress-test against the anti-generic bar. Keep "Beeond" in Latin within Hebrew text.

### Hero — claim with tension (pick/refine one)

**Option 1 — the pace claim**
- HE: **עידן ה-AI זז מהר. הנוכחות הדיגיטלית שלכם צריכה לזוז מהר יותר.**
- EN: **The AI era moves fast. Your digital presence has to move faster.**

**Option 2 — the whole-footprint claim**
- HE: **כל הנוכחות הדיגיטלית שלכם — מנוהלת כמו נחיל אחד, לעבר מטרה אחת.**
- EN: **Your entire digital footprint — run like one swarm, toward one goal.**

**Option 3 — the "where buyers look" claim**
- HE: **הלקוחות שלכם — והבינה המלאכותית שהם שואלים — מחפשים אתכם עכשיו. שיהיה מה למצוא.**
- EN: **Your buyers — and the AI they ask — are looking for you right now. Give them something to find.**

**Hero subline (one sentence, shared intent):**
- HE: Beeond מנהלת את כל הנוכחות הדיגיטלית שלכם — כל ערוץ שבו קונים מחפשים ומעריכים ספקים — כנחיל אחד מתואם, בקצב של השוק.
- EN: Beeond runs your whole digital presence — every channel buyers use to find and judge vendors — as one coordinated swarm, at the speed the market moves.

**Hero CTA:** `קבלו ניתוח נוכחות חינם` / `Get your free Footprint Audit`
**Hero micro:** `בלי מצגת. בלי פיץ'. מבט אמיתי על איפה יש פערים בנוכחות שלכם.` / `No deck. No pitch. A real look at where your footprint has gaps.`

### Proof strip
- HE: `11 ערוצים, מנוהלים כמערכת אחת · נחיל אחד עושה את הווליום · שבועות, לא חודשים.`
- EN: `11 channels, run as one system · one swarm doing the volume · weeks, not months.`
*(Until real client numbers exist, the proof is the founders' credibility + the system's scope — stated honestly.)*

### The problem (one beat)
- HE: **היום לא מספיק "להיות באינטרנט". צריך להופיע — ולשכנע — בכל מקום שבו בודקים אתכם. כולל התשובות של ה-AI.**
- EN: **Being "online" isn't enough anymore. You have to show up — and convince — everywhere you're evaluated. Including inside the AI's answers.**

### What Beeond does (3 jobs, as outcomes)
- HE: `שיִמצאו אתכם בכל מקום` · `מנוע תוכן שבאמת מייצר` · `אינטליגנציה שמצטברת`
- EN: `Be found everywhere buyers look` · `A content engine that actually ships` · `Intelligence that compounds`

### The swarm showpiece (the differentiator line)
- HE: **הצוות שאתם עוד לא יכולים לגייס — כבר עובד.** ...ואז: **האוטומציה עושה את הווליום. האנשים עושים את הטעם.**
- EN: **The team you can't hire yet is already working.** …then: **Automation does the volume. Humans do the taste.**

### How it works (de-risk)
- HE: **בונים את הנוכחות בשכבות — לא מדליקים הכול ביום הראשון.** יסוד (שבועות 1–4) → מנוע תוכן (4–8) → הגברה (8–12).
- EN: **We build your footprint in layers — we don't light everything up on day one.** Foundation (wks 1–4) → Content engine (4–8) → Amplification (8–12).

### Honesty / fit
- HE: `זה בשבילכם אם —` / `ובכנות, פחות בשבילכם אם —`
- EN: `It's for you if —` / `And honestly, not for you if —`

### Final CTA
- HE: **הקונים שלכם מחפשים. בואו נדאג שהם ימצאו אתכם.** → `התחילו את ניתוח הנוכחות`
- EN: **Your buyers are looking. Let's make sure they find you.** → `Start your Footprint Audit`

## The wizard microcopy (see `06-SITE-ARCHITECTURE`)

Write the wizard to feel like the *first taste of the product*, warm and specific:
- Step 1 ask: `איפה המותג שלכם חי online?` / `Where does your brand live online?`
- Reveal loading: `סורקים את הנוכחות שלכם…` / `Reading your footprint…` (honest, not fake-precise)
- Capture: `רוצים את הניתוח המלא + 30 דקות תכנון עם ירדן ואדם?` / `Want the full audit + a 30-min plan with Yarden & Adam?`
- Success: warm confirmation, sets expectation of what happens next and when.
