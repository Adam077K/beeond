01 · Logo — figurative device (registrable) + distinctive wordmark

Honey field · inverted device

The hive + bee device is the primary distinctive, registrable figurative mark — one locked, friendly top-down bee (striped body, wings, antennae; no face), identical at every size. The applied wordmark sets the “o” as a honeycomb cell, so applied brand reads as a designed logotype, never stock Rubik. (Premium “ee”-cells ligature stays a flagged type-designer enhancement — out of applied use until vectored, to avoid a “Beoond” misread.)
