# Beeond — Startup Overview

> The single consolidated snapshot of the business, the idea, and the startup as it stands. Pulls together every decision made across the founding discovery + Wave 1/2 work into one readable source of truth.
> **As of:** 2026-07-03 · Authored by CEO (ceo-beeond-discovery) · Status: strategy + brand + offer locked; pre-revenue; website not yet built by choice.

---

## 1. The one-paragraph version

**Beeond** is a **bilingual (Hebrew + English) done-for-you marketing & growth agency for B2B / SaaS / tech companies.** It runs a client's **entire digital presence** — search, AI-search, content, social, paid, email, and more — as one coordinated **swarm of AI agents + two expert founders**, delivering agency-quality work at a speed and price no traditional agency can match. Two founders (Yarden — marketing/growth; Adam — AI agents/swarms/systems) deliver mostly manually today and automate the flows over time. It has two warm, unsigned customers and no brand/site/revenue yet — the strategy, offer, pricing, and brand design are done; the next step is selling the first paid engagement.

---

## 2. The idea & the name

- **The bee/swarm metaphor:** wild bees move as a coordinated swarm toward one goal in the hive. Beeond's agents + humans all work together toward **one goal: the client's digital presence.**
- **"Beyond":** signals the future of how marketing gets done — AI that lowers price, ships faster, adapts faster.
- **The core belief:** the winning model isn't "an agency that uses AI" or "a DIY AI tool" — it's a **swarm that runs your whole presence toward one goal, with humans owning the taste.** Integration + coordination is the edge, not any single channel.
- **The wedge question, resolved:** founder chose **no single wedge** — sell the whole presence — made deliverable through packaging + phased onboarding (§5).

---

## 3. Who it's for

- **ICP:** B2B / SaaS / tech. Initial pull = **B2B service businesses & agencies** (possible white-label/partner angle).
- **Market & language:** **Israel + international, Hebrew + English at parity** from day one.
- **The customer insight to honor:** SMBs fear AI marketing because they think it'll look generic. Beeond's promise is the opposite — **automation does the volume, humans do the taste.**
- **Current customers:** two — **warm & likely, scope undefined.** ⚠️ Names/industries/needs/budgets still not captured (biggest open gap — §10).

## 5. The offer

**Whole presence, made deliverable via tiered packages + a 60–90-day phased onboarding** (Foundation → Content engine → Amplification — never light up every channel week one).

**The funnel (LOCKED):**
`Free Footprint Audit` (lead magnet — diagnosis only, 5–7 gaps, no build) → `Paid Pilot Sprint` ($1,500–$2,500 one-time, ~30 days: deep audit + one quick win implemented + 90-day roadmap + readout call) → `Growth Retainer`. Fallback: Pilot → Starter → Growth.

**Tiers (prices are estimates, pre-publication, fully reversible):**

| Tier | Price (USD / ₪) | Founder hrs/mo | Role |
|------|-----------------|----------------|------|
| Pilot Sprint | $1,500–2,500 one-time | ~12 total | Paid on-ramp / first "yes" |
| Starter | $2,000–3,500/mo | ~10 | Foundations; fallback |
| **Growth** | **$4,500–7,000/mo** | ~28 | **Primary profit tier** |
| Scale | $9,000–15,000/mo | ~50 (at ceiling) | Full footprint; capacity-gated (max 2 concurrent) |

- **Service catalog (LOCKED):** CORE = GEO/AI-search, SEO content, schema, rank tracking, website, landing/CRO, paid ads, LinkedIn organic, digital PR/backlinks, Reddit/blogs, YouTube, short-form video, email lifecycle + deliverability, reporting, funnel insight, brand monitoring, case studies, founder personal branding; Scale adds newsletter, CRM/automation, attribution, G2/Capterra. **ADD-ONS ONLY:** Google Business Profile, reviews, citations/NAP, speed-to-lead, SMS (local-flavored, demoted for a B2B ICP).
- Detail: `docs/04-features/OFFER_SPEC.md` (per-tier deliverables, cadence, definition-of-done, all fit within hour budgets).

## 6. Business model & economics (estimates)

- **Model:** flat monthly retainer by tier + one-time paid pilot. Recurring MRR.
- **Capacity ceiling (2 founders, manual, pre-automation):** ~7–8 clients → ~$42–52k MRR ($500–630k ARR). Automation lifts this.
- **Ramen-profitable:** 2 Growth clients ($11,500 MRR), targeted ~Q3 2026.
- **Gross margin (tool costs only):** 83–96%.
- **Discounts:** 15% annual; first 2 clients get 20–25% intro (months 1–3) for case-study rights; pilot fee waived on a 6-month Growth signing.
- **Ad spend:** client-direct for Wave 1 (recommended).
- Detail: `docs/01-foundation/BUSINESS_MODEL.md`

## 7. Why we win (moat — honest)

Thin today (new agency). Built via:
1. **Swarm tech (Adam)** — proprietary agent orchestration that compounds: every codified flow lowers cost + raises speed vs. competitors.
2. **Founder pairing** — rare combo of real marketing judgment (Yarden) + AI systems depth (Adam).
3. **Speed + price** — a wedge, not a moat alone (copyable); defensible only fused with the swarm + whole-footprint integration.
4. **Integration / data** — running the *whole* presence creates switching cost + cross-channel data advantage over point-solution rivals.

**Competitive white space (confirmed):** no current player occupies whole-footprint + AI-swarm + **bilingual HE+EN**. Closest rival GrowthSpree ($3k/mo) is US-only, paid-skewed. GEO-as-a-service is commoditizing (validates *not* making GEO the wedge). Honest caveat: "AI-native agency" is a crowded phrase; no track record yet.
Detail: `docs/02-competitive/COMPETITIVE_LANDSCAPE.md`

## 8. Brand & delivery model

- **Brand vibe:** warm & natural (bee/hive/honey) × futuristic tech × friendly. Concept: **"Warm Hive Futurism."** Deliberately anti-template (honey/cream/espresso palette, *no* SaaS blue/purple) to prove the "AI that doesn't look generic" promise.
- **Identity:** distinctive composite mark (hive-cell + friendly bee device) + designed logotype (honeycomb-cell "o"); Rubik (true HE+EN parity). Full bilingual one-page landing designed (EN LTR + HE RTL), passed independent design-critic (anti-generic PASS) after one revision. Design is build-ready but **not built** and **not launched**.
- **Delivery now:** mostly manual, AI-assisted, by the two founders → codify flows → progressively replace with swarms; humans keep strategy/creative/comms.
- Detail: `docs/05-marketing/BRAND_AND_LANDING_DESIGN.md` + `brand-assets/`
