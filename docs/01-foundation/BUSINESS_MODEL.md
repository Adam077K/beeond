## Land-and-Expand Funnel

```
[1] FREE Footprint Audit  (~1–2 hrs founder time, no cost to prospect)
     Surface-level tool scan: 5–7 identified gaps in SEO, GEO, content, LinkedIn.
     CMO's recommended lead CTA. Purpose: generate a qualified lead and earn the next meeting.
     Does NOT include implementation or a detailed roadmap — that is the Pilot.

          ↓ "Here are 5 critical gaps holding you back. Want us to fix the biggest one and
             show you exactly what a full engagement looks like?"

[2] PAID Pilot Sprint  ($1,500–$2,500 one-time, ~12 hrs over 30 days)
     Deep-dive audit + one channel quick-win implemented + 90-day Growth roadmap.
     Purpose: de-risk commitment for price-sensitive prospects; earn trust before asking
     for a monthly retainer; generate early case-study data. Profitable on its own.

          ↓ "We moved the needle on [X] in 30 days. Growth tier runs all of this every month."

[3] Growth Retainer  ($4,500–$7,000/mo) ← the anchor / profit tier
     Core footprint running monthly. Upsell target for 60%+ of pilot completions. (assumed)

          ↓ (for clients not yet ready for Growth after the pilot)

[3b] Starter Retainer  ($2,000–$3,500/mo) ← fallback / stepping stone
     Foundation footprint. Used when prospect is sold but genuinely can't commit to Growth.
     Target: upgrade to Growth within 3–6 months.

          ↓

[4] Scale Retainer  ($9,000–$15,000/mo)
     Full footprint. Natural upgrade once Growth client is growing and wants everything.
```

**Distinction rule:** The free audit is a diagnostic (we tell them what's wrong). The paid pilot is a prescription + first treatment (we show them the fix and build the plan). They must feel different — different scope, different depth, different deliverable, different meeting format.

## Tier Definitions

### Pilot Sprint — "See what we can do. No strings."

**Target persona:** Price-sensitive B2B service business or agency that has seen the free audit and wants to see Beeond's work before committing to a monthly retainer. The free audit created urgency; the pilot creates proof.

**Format:** 30-day engagement. Upfront, fixed-fee.

**Deliverables:**
- In-depth audit (3–4× deeper than the free audit): technical SEO scan, GEO/AI-search readiness check, LinkedIn profile and content gap analysis, competitor gap snapshot
- One quick-win implemented: founders choose the highest-impact fix from the audit (e.g., schema markup deployed, 2 LinkedIn posts published and scheduled, top 10 technical SEO issues resolved, or GEO entity setup complete)
- 90-day Growth roadmap: channel-by-channel prioritized plan with specific actions, timelines, and what the Growth retainer would execute
- 60-minute readout call with both founders (Yarden + Adam)

**Pilot pricing:** $1,500–$2,500 one-time (₪5,475–₪9,125). Effective rate: ~$125–$210/hr (estimate). Gross margin at tool costs only: ~92% (estimate). The pilot is profitable even when it does not convert.

**Conversion goal:** 60% of completed pilots → Growth retainer (assumed; no data yet). Monitor this rate from first pilot completed.

**Capacity rule:** No more than 2 active pilots running simultaneously. Pilot slots are opened only when retainer client load permits (i.e., pilots consume ~12 hrs of the 220 hrs/month delivery budget; holding 24 hrs for 2 pilots leaves 196 hrs for retainer work).

**NOT included:** Ongoing monthly delivery, ad management, content series, or email lifecycle. Those begin only once a retainer is signed.

---

### Starter — "Get found. Get credible."

**Target persona:** B2B service business or agency, < 10 employees, first systematic digital presence effort, or: post-pilot client who wants to maintain momentum but isn't ready for Growth spend. Budget-conscious. Expected to upgrade to Growth within 3–6 months.

**Services included (monthly delivery):**
- GEO / AI-search visibility maintenance (setup done in pilot or onboarding; monthly entity and schema updates)
- SEO content engine: 2–3 pieces/month (long-form blog/resource)
- Website SEO: quarterly audit cycle + prioritized fix implementation
- LinkedIn organic: 4–6 posts/month (founder voice + company page)
- Rank tracking + monthly reporting dashboard
- Brand monitoring (alerts + monthly summary)

**NOT included at Starter:** Paid ads, YouTube, email lifecycle, short-form video, landing-page builds, PR/backlinks.

---

### Growth — "Build the engine. Fill the funnel." (Primary tier)

**Target persona:** B2B/SaaS company that has completed a pilot (or comes in via referral), has proven traction, and wants marketing running as a reliable revenue engine. 10–50 employees. Realistic $5K+/month marketing budget.

**Services included (everything in Starter, plus):**
- Paid ads management: LinkedIn Ads + Google (ad spend billed separately at cost pass-through)
- Email lifecycle: onboarding sequence + monthly nurture (up to 4 emails/month)
- Landing pages / CRO: up to 2 new pages or experiments/month
- Digital PR + backlink building: 2–4 outreach placements/month
- Expanded social: LinkedIn-led + one additional channel (Twitter/X or Reddit)
- Case studies & social-proof assets: 1/quarter
- Founder-led / personal branding content: 2–3 posts/month per founder

---

### Scale — "Full swarm. Full footprint."

**Target persona:** Scale-up B2B/SaaS, 50+ employees, aggressive growth, needs every channel running without internal marketing overhead.

**Services included (everything in Growth, plus):**
- YouTube content: 2 videos/month (strategy, scripting, production coordination)
- Short-form video: 4–6 clips/month (repurposed from long-form)
- Webinars / virtual events: planning + promotion (1/quarter)
- Newsletter as owned media asset: weekly edition
- Lead magnets / gated content: 1 new asset/quarter
- Marketing automation + CRM integration: HubSpot or equivalent setup + maintenance
- Full attribution / pipeline analytics: marketing → revenue tracing
- Review-site presence: G2 / Capterra / Trustpilot management
- Dedicated weekly strategy call + account lead (Yarden)

## Capacity Model

> Two founders, mostly manual delivery. Delivery-hour model.

**Monthly hours budget:**
- Gross working hours: 2 founders × ~160 hrs = 320 hrs/month
- Overhead (sales + automation-build + admin + free audits): ~100 hrs/month (estimate)
- **Deliverable hours: ~220 hrs/month (estimate)**

**Hours per engagement per month (estimate):**

| Engagement | Hrs | Clients at full capacity | Max revenue impact |
|-----------|-----|------------------------|-------------------|
| Free audit | 1–2 (one-time) | Not a capacity constraint | $0 — lead gen only |
| Pilot Sprint | ~12 (one-time, 30 days) | 2 active simultaneously (max) | ~$4,000/mo one-time if 2 run/mo |
| Starter retainer | ~10/mo | 22 | $60,500/mo (estimate) |
| Growth retainer | ~28/mo | 7 | $40,250/mo (estimate) |
| Scale retainer | ~50/mo | 4 | $48,000/mo (estimate) |

**Capacity rule for pilots:** Reserve no more than 24 hrs/month (2 active pilots) for pilot sprints. The remaining 196 hrs serve retainer clients. Never let pilots backlog — if more than 2 pilots would be active simultaneously, queue the next one for the following month.

**Realistic mixed-portfolio ceiling (before automation):**

| Mix | Clients | Hrs used | MRR (recurring) | ARR |
|-----|---------|---------|-----------------|-----|
| 2× active pilots + 4× Growth + 2× Starter | 6 retainer + 2 pilots | ~216 hrs | $43,500/mo + ~$4,000 one-time | ~$522K ARR (estimate) |

**Interpretation:** Natural MRR ceiling pre-automation: **~$42K–$52K/month** in recurring retainer revenue. Automation breaks this ceiling upward by reducing hours-per-client.

## Unit Economics

| Engagement | Revenue | Direct cost (estimate) | Gross Margin % (estimate) |

| Pilot Sprint (mid $2,000) | $2,000 one-time | ~$150 | 92% |
| Starter (mid $2,750/mo) | $2,750/mo | ~$468/mo | 83% |
| Growth (mid $5,750/mo) | $5,750/mo | ~$468/mo | 92% |

*Direct cost per retainer client = (fixed $1,340 / assumed 5 clients) + $200 variable. Pilot cost is fixed-fee tool time only.*

**Pilot conversion economics:**
- Target conversion: 60% of pilots → Growth retainer (assumed; benchmark-based)
- Pilots run per Growth client acquired: 1.7 on average
- Pilot revenue earned per Growth client acquired: $3,333 (= 1.7 × $2,000 mid)
- Effective CAC for Growth clients via pilot channel: negative (pilot stage earns revenue and qualifies the lead simultaneously)

**LTV — Growth tier base case:**
- Formula: `ARPU × Gross Margin % ÷ Monthly Churn Rate`
- ARPU: $5,750 (estimate, Growth mid)
- Gross Margin: 92% (estimate)
- Monthly Churn: 4% (estimate — agency benchmark; no cohort data)
- **LTV = $5,750 × 0.92 ÷ 0.04 = $132,250 (estimate)**
- Avg client lifetime: 25 months (estimate)

**LTV:CAC sensitivity (Growth tier via pilot funnel):**

| Scenario | Monthly Churn | CAC | LTV | LTV:CAC |
|----------|--------------|-----|-----|---------|
| Downside | 6% | $1,000 | $87,973 (estimate) | 88:1 (estimate) |
| Base | 4% | ~$0 net (pilot earns revenue) | $132,250 (estimate) | >> 3:1 |
| Upside | 2% | ~$0 net | $264,500 (estimate) | >> 3:1 |

*Even the downside scenario is well above the 3:1 healthy floor. The binding constraint is capacity, not unit economics.*

## Pricing Strategy

**Model:** Paid one-time pilot as on-ramp; flat monthly retainer by scope tier thereafter. No per-seat or usage billing. Services business (not SaaS).

**On-ramp rationale (why Pilot over a permanent lite tier):**
A permanent lite tier (~$1,000/mo) was evaluated and rejected. To match 2 Growth clients' MRR ($11,500), a lite tier would require ~12 lite clients — 12 client relationships and ~58 hrs/month of delivery for the same revenue as 2 Growth clients consuming ~56 hrs. The operational complexity of 12 relationships is the same as 2 Growth clients but with far more churn exposure (small agencies often churn fast). The Paid Pilot solves the "first yes" problem without creating a permanent low-value client segment. Lite-tier clients who want ongoing engagement after the pilot should slot into Starter, not a fourth, cheaper tier.

**Price anchor (what clients compare against):**

| Alternative | Monthly cost (estimate) | Beeond advantage |
|------------|------------------------|-----------------|
| In-house B2B marketing manager (Israel) | ₪15,000–₪25,000/mo salary only (~$4,100–$6,850) — excludes overhead, ramp, management | Whole footprint vs. one generalist; AI-native speed |
| Traditional Israeli agency (partial scope) | ₪15,000–₪40,000/mo for 3–4 channels (estimate) | Full footprint + AI speed vs. siloed channels |
| Traditional US agency (full scope B2B) | $8,000–$20,000/mo (estimate) | Comparable scope; Beeond faster + AI-native |
| Point-solution tools (SEO + social + ads) | $3,000–$8,000/mo in subscriptions alone (estimate) | Coordinated execution vs. fragmented tools |

**Talking point for price-sensitive B2B agencies:** "A $2,000 sprint is less than one day of agency fees at most Israeli agencies, and you walk away with a full roadmap and proof we can execute — before committing to anything monthly."

**Pricing evolution as automation lands:**
- Phase 1 (now, manual): Fixed retainer prices above. Pilot as the primary on-ramp.
- Phase 2 (6–12 months, partial automation): Same prices; fewer hours-per-client → can serve more clients; improve margin without raising prices.
- Phase 3 (12–24 months, automation-led): Consider tier price increases if demonstrated results justify it. Add a performance-linked component to Scale tier.
- Phase 4 (scale): Hybrid retainer + outcome fee. Possible Enterprise/Custom tier for large ACV.

## Open Questions for Founders

> Still required before pricing goes external.

1. **Warm prospect budgets.** What is each prospect currently spending on marketing (if anything)? This determines whether to pitch Pilot → Growth or Pilot → Starter for each one.
2. **Founder living-cost floor.** Is $5,000/month per founder accurate? This directly shifts the ramen-profitable milestone date.
3. **Cash runway.** How many months can both founders run on personal savings before needing MRR to cover living costs? Sets the urgency level.
4. **ILS vs. USD invoicing.** For Israeli clients, will Beeond invoice in ILS or USD? Affects payment tooling and currency risk.
5. **Pilot waiver offer.** Confirm: if a prospect signs a 6-month Growth retainer within 14 days of pilot delivery, waive the pilot fee? (Recommended — removes the last friction point at the conversion moment.)
6. **Ad spend flow.** Client-direct (client pays platforms directly) or Beeond-fronted (Beeond advances, client reimburses)? Recommend client-direct in Wave 1 to avoid cash-flow timing risk.
7. **CPO scope confirmation.** CPO to validate service catalog per tier before first proposal is sent to a prospect.

## Reversibility

**Reversibility: easy.** Pricing is proposed, not published or billed. No clients have been quoted or signed. All prices and the pilot structure can be adjusted without any external consequence. Once the first pilot is quoted to a prospect in writing, that specific price is effectively locked for that engagement; future engagements can still differ.
